NISE3

NISE3

NISE3 is a skills platform that is being developed for Bangladesh, where youth (Student, Skill Seeker, Apprentice, Job seekers, Entrepreneurs, Migrant worker) can explore and expand their knowledge base. NISE3 will help the Youth to enhance their skills, find them a suitable job according to their skills, provide them with business idea with proper guideline and financial grant. The portal will also help the youth to seek for jobs and education in foreign land and provide the necessary guideline for migration. NISE3 is a guidance system and a match making platform for the Youth in need for job and academic assistance. NISE3 will also be used as a monitoring platform for the government.

Start to Finish Service Tracking System(S2F Service Tracker)

Start to Finish Service Tracking System(S2F Service Tracker)

The Rights, Empowerment and Cohesion for Rural and Urban Fijians (REACH) Project, UNDP Pacific Office has developed a concept the “Start-to-Finish (S2F) Service Tracker System”, the purpose of which is to track services provided by the relevant government stakeholders, and in particular the Ministry of Women, Children and Poverty Alleviation from end-to-end in Fiji during outreach activities undertaken through Project REACH. Currently, under Project REACH service delivery is being undertaken utilising three mobile service delivery teams using buses, which are moving around different – typically remote – locations in Fiji to provide social, economic and legal services.

  • Make government services Track able, Traceable, Accountable and Transparent.
  • Reduce time, difficulty and costs of obtaining government services
  • Bring all government services under one umbrella
Development for: UNDP Bangladesh & UNDP Pacific Office
Financial Inclusion in Bangladesh

Financial Inclusion in Bangladesh

A2i will develop a knowledge management website which will be a future DFS/Financial Inclusion central knowledge hub and access point on financial inclusion in Bangladesh. The portal will guide the future research and knowledge management activity to create a fact base for advocacy for innovative and competitive policy. A2i will also establish partnership and collaboration with necessary organization, researcher & academia to establish the knowledge website.

Purpose

  • Provide access to up-to-date data of digital financial industry
  • Source for data, research and insights on financial inclusion in Bangladesh
  • Disseminates data-driven evidence-based insights to FSPs and policy makers to encourage them to develop innovative solutions that meet appropriate needs of their users
  • Bring evidence at the heart of policy formulation and product development in the financial sector
  • Provide market analysis and gather market information identify barriers to and opportunities for financial inclusion and raise awareness
  • Harness data to spur innovations that can help address the financial barriers of marginalized groups.
