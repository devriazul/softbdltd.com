Search Engine Optimization (SEO)

What good is a website if it cannot be found in the search engines? What good is a website if the user experience is poor and visitors leave the website immediately? In some cases, not having a website is probably better than having a website design that is so poor as to leave visitors with a negative view of your organization.

Search Engine Optimization (SEO) service is much more than a mere commitment towards working upon your site. Search engine users hardly ever go beyond the second or third page of search engine rankings. The audience today has become so net savvy that more than 80% of the website visitors come from the search engines. SoftBD Ltd provides your website with the following features in our Search engine optimization services package:

On-Page Search Engine Optimization:
Placing content on the pages includes much more than writing and copy pasting. To be able to reach your audience, you need to manipulate the content on the website, keep it fresh, readable, interesting. Good content, articles, blogs, all need to be such that not only render information and knowledge to the reader but also hold their interest as well as that of the search engines.
  • Content
  • Title, description, images
  • Page formatting
  • Categorisation
  • Site-map
  • URL structuring
  • Internal links
  • Keywords research
  • Tag hierarchy(h1-h6)
  • Robots txt

Off-Page Search Engine Optimisation:
Off page SEO are the techniques that are implemented to increases the website's ranking on search engines. This is a process to get external links for your website and add to its online value. A professional web development company trains its team to carefully implement off page SEO along with on page SEO to yield best results. It also requires continuous efforts. Our off - page SEO techniques include:
  • Link building
  • Social media, Facebook page, twitter, Google Plus, Pinterest etc
  • Directory submissions
  • Search engine submission like Google, Yahoo, Bing
  • Article submission
  • Classified add
  • Social bookmarking
  • Submit site in Bangladeshi directory
  • Blog comment
  • Yellow pages submission
